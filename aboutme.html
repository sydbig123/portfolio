About Me

I am working towards my bachelors degree in Human-Centered Design and Development (HCDD) as well as a minor in Psychology. Currently, I am looking for an internship for summer 2024.

Organizations

During my senior year (2023-2024), I participated in THON as a Rules & Regulations Committee Member. THON is a student-run philanthropy that is raises funds for pediatric cancer research while also providing emotional and financial support to those affected by pediatric cancer. As a Rules & Regulations Committee Member I assisted in signing people on and off the event level during THON Weekend and ensured that spectators in the stands followed fire safety regulations. In addition to this role, I also served as the EMS Specialist for my committee. This entailed educating my fellow committee members on what to do in the event of a medical emergency. Unfortunately, a medical emergency occurred while my committee was on shift during THON Weekend. However, I can proudly say that all the members of my committee handled the emergency very well and remembered how to properly manage the situation.

To learn more about THON: thon.org
