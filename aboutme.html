About Me

Hello! I am Sydney Biggar, a graduate of The Pennsylvania State University ('24). In December 2024 I received my Bachelor's degree in Human-Centered Design and Development (HCDD), with a focus in Psychology, along with a minor in Psychology. HCDD is a combination of software development and human-computer interaction (HCI). My background in coding, HCI, and psychology enables me to design, build, and test intuitive, user-friendly, and visually appealing applications.

Organizations

During my senior year (2023-2024), I participated in THON as a Rules & Regulations Committee Member. THON is a student-run philanthropy that raises funds for pediatric cancer research in addition to providing emotional and financial support to families affected by pediatric cancer. As a Rules & Regulations Committee Member I assisted in signing people on and off the event level during THON Weekend and ensured that spectators in the stands followed fire safety regulations. In addition to this role, I served as the EMS Specialist for my committee. This entailed educating my fellow committee members on what to do in the event of a medical emergency. Unfortunately, during THON weekend a medical emergency did occur in my committee's section while we were on shift. However, I can proudly say that the members of my committee handled the emergency very well and remembered how to properly manage the situation.
